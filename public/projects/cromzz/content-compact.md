## Overview

Cromzz is a fitness-tech startup that supports gym users through data-driven insights and motivation. The product combines a physical tracking device with a mobile app to transform complex workout data into clear, personalized metrics. Beyond performance tracking, Cromzz uses gamification to increase retention through challenges, progress tracking, and friendly competition.

## Problem

Most fitness apps either oversimplify tracking or overwhelm users with raw, hard-to-interpret data. While gym-goers generate large amounts of performance data, it is often poorly visualized and disconnected from real training goals. Combined with low motivation and lack of social engagement, this leads to low retention and early abandonment of fitness apps.

## Challenges

The main challenge was translating complex workout data into insights that feel personal, motivating, and easy to understand across different fitness levels. At the same time, gamification needed to encourage progress without creating pressure or unhealthy competition. An additional complexity came from integrating hardware and software into a single, seamless user experience.

## Discovery & Platform Audit

The discovery phase focused on analyzing fitness apps and tracking platforms, with an emphasis on data visualization and disengagement points. This research revealed the need to simplify heavy data into a small set of meaningful metrics. As a result, performance data was distilled into four core indicators: 
* **Evolution** – progress over time compared to past performance
* **Smoothness** – precision and execution quality
* **Endurance** – consistency of execution speed across repetitions
* **Control** – rhythm stability between reps

## User, Roles & Business Alignment
![Competition screen](/projects/cromzz/ramat.png#right)

The primary users were active gym-goers seeking clarity, motivation, and visible progress. From a business perspective, retention and engagement were the main success metrics. To reduce the risks of unhealthy competition, comparisons were limited to friends added by the user, encouraging fair and controlled motivation.

## Wireframing & Interaction Design
![Competition screen](/projects/cromzz/competition.png#right)

Wireframes explored multiple ways of presenting data-heavy content, focusing on fast readability in a gym environment. Different layout types were introduced to support both detailed analysis and quick scanning between sets. Testing showed that personal points needed to take priority over rankings to reinforce self-improvement over competition.

##  Data Visualization & Gamification Design

Raw workout data was transformed into intuitive visual components that communicate progress at a glance. Gamification elements such as points, streaks, and challenges were integrated carefully to support motivation without distracting from fitness goals. The focus remained on clarity, emotional feedback, and a clear sense of achievement.

##  Visual Design & Consistency

The visual language was designed to feel energetic and performance-driven while remaining readable in low-light gym environments. Color was used intentionally to highlight progress and improvements. Consistency across the product ensured a smooth and predictable experience.

##  Validation & Iteration

The experience was continuously refined through feedback and usability testing. Iterations focused on improving data clarity, reducing cognitive load, and ensuring that competitive features felt inclusive and encouraging. This process helped create a sustainable experience that supports long-term engagement.
